1. A rectangular swimming pool measures 50 feet long by 20 feet wide. The depth slants uniformly from 3 feet in the shallow end to 5 feet in the deep end. What is the total volume of this pool in gallons?

2. A rectangular public swimming pool measures 60 feet long by 30 feet wide. Over a hot weekend, evaporation causes the water level to drop straight down by exactly 2 inches. How many total gallons of water did the pool lose?

3. Your pool volume is 20,000 gallons. Your current Total Alkalinity reading is 70 ppm, and your target is 100 ppm. How many pounds of Sodium Bicarbonate do you need to add?

4. A commercial pool has a total calculated volume of 72,000 gallons. State codes mandate a minimum 6-hour turnover rate for this facility type. What is the required design flow rate in Gallons Per Minute (GPM)?

5. You check the flow meter on a 45,000-gallon public pool and read an active flow rate of 125 Gallons Per Minute (GPM). What is the actual turnover rate of the pool in hours?

6. A pool system operates at a flow rate of 120 Gallons Per Minute (GPM). It utilizes high-rate sand filtration, which possesses a maximum design filtration rate of 15 GPM per square foot. What is the minimum required filter surface area in square feet?

7. A facility uses a cartridge system providing a total filter surface area of 300 square feet. The filter media rate is 0.375 GPM per square foot, what is the maximum allowable flow rate in GPM for this filter system?

8. A 50,000-gallon aquatic center pool has a Total Chlorine (TC) reading of 4.2 ppm and a Free Chlorine (FC) of 3.4 ppm. How many gallons of Sodium Hypochlorite (12%) must be added to reach exact breakpoint chlorination?

9. Calculate the Saturation Index (SI) for a commercial hot tub feature with the following readings: pH 7.6, Temp 102°F, Calcium Hardness 300 ppm, Total Alkalinity 150 ppm, and TDS 1200 ppm. What is the saturation index?

10. A 60,000-gallon pool has a Total Chlorine reading of 2.3 ppm and a Free Chlorine reading of 1.5 ppm. Calculate how many pounds of Calcium Hypochlorite (67%) are needed to achieve breakpoint chlorination.
